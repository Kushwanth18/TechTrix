About
us :

TECHTRIX is part of college-wide Fest Exuberanza hosted by MRCET. It is a hub of Technical and Non-Technical events.We create a platform for students to present their technical and creative skills. All of the activities culminate in a large two-day event at MRCET Hyderabad which majorly attracts students, educational institutions, companies and the general public. We organize electrifying and exuberant events that allow students of various departments and colleges to participate and win exciting prizes. The fest also contains mouth watering food stalls and games organized by individuals that showcase entrepreneurial excellence.
